Reimbursement Policy


1. Purpose

The reimbursement policy ensures employees are compensated for approved business-related expenses incurred while performing official duties.


2. Eligible Expenses

Business travel (air, train, cab, fuel)
Meals during official travel
Accommodation for approved overnight stays
Other expenses explicitly approved by the reporting manager


3. Limits & Guidelines

Domestic travel per diem: Up to $50 per day
Travel should follow the most economical and reasonable option
Luxury or personal expenses are not reimbursable


4. Claim Submission

Claims must be submitted within 30 days of incurring the expense
Original receipts or valid digital invoices are mandatory
Claims should be submitted through the designated expense management system


5. Approval & Payment

Reimbursements are subject to manager and finance approval
Approved claims are processed within the standard payroll or reimbursement cycle
