Work From Home (WFH) Policy


1. Purpose

This policy outlines the framework for employees to work remotely while maintaining productivity and collaboration.


2. Eligibility

Employees may work from home up to 8 days per month
WFH is subject to prior approval from the reporting manager
Certain roles may require on-site presence and may not be eligible


3. Working Hours

Core working hours: 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM
Employees must be reachable via official communication channels during core hours
Total working hours should align with standard company expectations


4. Expectations & Responsibilities

Employees must ensure a stable internet connection and secure work environment
Confidential company data must be handled responsibly
Regular participation in meetings and updates is mandatory


5. Compliance

Abuse of WFH privileges may result in revocation of remote work access
The company reserves the right to modify or withdraw this policy based on business needs
